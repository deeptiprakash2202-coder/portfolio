note

on not knowing

for a long time, i thought clarity came before action.

i thought people who achieved great things had a perfect plan.

the older i get, the less i believe that's true.

most people are figuring things out as they go.

they follow a question. then another.

right now, i'm interested in software engineering, psychology, philosophy, startups, and a hundred other things.

sometimes that feels exciting.

sometimes it feels confusing.

maybe the goal isn't to know exactly where i'm going.

maybe the goal is to stay curious enough to keep moving.

for now, that's enough.
